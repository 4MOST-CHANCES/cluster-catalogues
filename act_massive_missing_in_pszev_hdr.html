This table contains clusters that are among the 50 most massive in ACT which pass the CHANCES-Evolution sky and redshift cuts but are not part of the 50 most massive PSZ2 clusters, which we're using as the basis for our sample

Details:
  • (*) are the clusters I would consider including. This is based only on a quick look at the optical image or on prior information (e.g., a Frontier Fields cluster)
  • no image means it's not in any of the modern optical surveys
  • Chandra means there are Chandra sources nearby in NED
  • index refers to the index in the ACT catalogue; it may be useful when referring to clusters in this list
